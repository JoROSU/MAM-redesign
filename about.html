Values

People and Processes

The user-centered design means that we are a deeply empathetic persons and we're able to precisely express users' needs and expectations. We prefer a holistic approach to the multidisciplinary design process.

We Talk

During the initial meeting with a client, we are collecting basic information, user's requirement, and expectations. Very often we recognize the client's needs before himself. We set a list of questions that helps us to make a comprehensive image. We insist on intensive communication with the client during the design and development process and even after this period.

We Think

After we mapped requirements, services, visual style, and final product, we explore competition and existing resources. The next step is the concept proposal, technical solution, development plan, and timeline strategy. During this step, we will provide a formal offer with expected costs and necessary external services.

We Create

Depending on project scope and expected final product we create a team of designers, scenographers, developers or involve external experts. During this phase, we will intensively communicate with a client and follow agile methodology. Our approach is to work with a client, not for a client.

We Deliver

Delivering process includes product, documentation, manual and one-month customer support. Even if we don't maintenance a product, we stay in touch with our clients and follow their digital presence, growth and future needs. We are proud of clients' loyalty because they recommend us, share a positive experience with our agency and come back to us again.
